# Playbook: Content Creation

**Trigger:** `run content creation`
**Purpose:** Generate VYVE brand content across all channels in Lewis's authentic voice

---

## Claude's Instructions

When this playbook is triggered:

### Step 1 — Load Context
Read from master.md:
- Content pillars
- LinkedIn strategy and what is performing
- Hashtags
- Recent published content
- VYVE brand voice rules

### Step 2 — Ask Lewis
Ask which of the following content types is needed today:

1. **LinkedIn post** (thought leadership, story, stat-based)
2. **Blog article** (long-form, SEO-friendly, evidence-based)
3. **Email copy** (prospect outreach, newsletter, member comms)
4. **Social content** (shorter posts for Instagram / Twitter / X)
5. **Podcast talking points** (episode outline, key messages)
6. **Content calendar** (plan for a week or month)

### Step 3 — Generate Content

**LinkedIn post format:**
- Hook in the first line
- 3-5 short paragraphs
- End with a question or CTA
- 3-5 hashtags

**Blog article format:**
- SEO-friendly title
- 600-1200 words

**Email format:**
- Subject line options
- Short body, single CTA

### Step 4 — Review & Iterate

### Step 5 — Update Brain

## Brand Voice Rules
- Warm, human, evidence-based
- UK English
- "Members" not users
- No "Corporate Wellness"
- Tie to a pillar
