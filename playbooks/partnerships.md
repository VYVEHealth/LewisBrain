# Playbook: Partnerships

## When to Use
Partner outreach, relationship management, co-marketing, referral arrangements.

## Steps

### 1. Identify Target
- Employers (10-200 employees, HR-led wellbeing budget)
- Public sector bodies (NHS trusts, councils, universities)
- Charities / third sector (aligned social mission)
- Complementary services (EAPs, occupational health, benefits platforms)

### 2. Research
- Company size, sector, decision maker
- Existing wellbeing provision (gap analysis)
- CIC alignment (social mission fit)

### 3. Outreach
- Lead with shared mission, not product pitch
- CIC credibility for public/third sector
- Offer pilot (small group, 8 weeks, measurable outcomes)
- Always team@vyvehealth.co.uk as contact

### 4. Track
- Log in Command Centre > Partner Network
- Update HubSpot if applicable
- Set follow-up cadence (same as sales pipeline)

### 5. Report
- Update `brain/master.md` if a significant partnership is confirmed
- Log in changelog via brain sync
